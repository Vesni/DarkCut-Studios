Season 2 - 2025 Edition

SMILE BEFORE YOU DIE

A haunting tale set in a seemingly normal high school, forever changed by the tragic suicide of Samir, an 11th grader who couldn't bear the weight of academic failure. When a brave student sits in Samir's old seat, they uncover a chilling truth about academic pressure, mental health, and the real meaning of success. But the biggest twist? The investigator and the ghost are one and the same...
