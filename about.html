About "Smile Before You Die"

Smile Before You Die is an immersive 30-day journey through thriller, mystery, and comedy — a unique blend designed to keep you on the edge of your seat and cracking up simultaneously.

Every day reveals a new secret, a new twist, and a new laugh. This series is crafted for those who love a story that challenges the mind, teases the senses, and entertains in unexpected ways.

Our mission at DarkCut Studios is to redefine storytelling, merging dark suspense with humor to deliver a one-of-a-kind viewing experience that resonates long after the credits roll.

With top-notch production, a dedicated creative team, and a passionate fanbase, Smile Before You Die is more than a series — it's a community of thrill-seekers and laugh-lovers alike.
